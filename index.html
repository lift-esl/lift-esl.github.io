Useful Links

Good podcasts with transcripts. Advanced, but great for improving your listening skills since you can both listen to and read the text. Plus they are brilliant, interesting shows.

  • This American Life
  • Story Corps (these are short)
  • S-Town

100 Most Common English Verbs   The most frequently used verbs in English plus all the essential information about them: pronunciation, past tense and other verb forms.

VOA Let’s Learn English Level 1   A free, quality video course. Learn English while following the slightly goofy but likeable Anna on her adventures around Washington D.C.

Duolingo   A nice, free app for learning languages, including English. You can use the web site, or the app for Android or Apple. No courses yet for Farsi speakers unfortunately.
